A SwiftUI menu-bar app for macOS that competes in the Magnet/Rectangle slot, with an AI premium tier on top. Global hotkeys snap the front window to preset positions; a Claude-powered Smart Sessions feature auto-arranges multi-window layouts tailored to coding, design, writing, or research contexts. Privacy-first - only app names and screen dimensions cross the wire, never window content.

Ships the full indie-app revenue stack: App Store one-time purchase plus a StoreKit 2 subscription gating the AI features, with sandbox configuration and entitlement-gated views wired end-to-end. An actor-isolated API client pairs with a rule-based fallback so the UI feels instant while the LLM is in flight. A DEBUG-only sandbox (mock AI client, in-memory IAP overrides, paywall triggers, launch-arg-driven UI test mode) gives the app a level of testability rare in indie macOS work. Third stablemate in the CrazyAIApps line.
