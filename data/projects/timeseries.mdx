## About

A small Ruby gem (February 2013) that adds a `timeseries` mixin to MongoMapper documents for storing time-series data in MongoDB and querying by range and granularity (e.g. `resolution => 'day'`). Single-purpose utility, packaged as a standard gem.

## Why notable

- Published Ruby gem from the early MongoDB era — shows comfort with NoSQL data modeling and Ruby DSL design beyond Rails defaults.
- Narrow but real: solves the bucketed-resolution-rollup problem before it became standard infra.

## Architecture

- One module (`lib/timeseries.rb`) plus `lib/timeseries/version.rb`.
- Mixed into a MongoMapper document; fields declared via a `timeseries [...]` DSL with per-field resolution config.
- Backed by MongoDB via MongoMapper (`mongoid.yml` config present).
- RSpec spec (`spec/timeseries_spec.rb`).
