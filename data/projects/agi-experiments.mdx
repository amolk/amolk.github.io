## About

A multi-year personal research sandbox investigating top-down approaches to AGI: building the simplest "minimal" agent first, then iterating capability. Notebooks span predictive coding, free-energy minimization, quantized distribution learning, attractor dynamics, active dendrite models, sparse spiking ensembles, and self-organizing maps. Python/PyTorch with wandb logging in later experiments. Status: paused as ideas migrated into focused projects (Pattern Machine, carapace-intelligence, fly).

## Why notable

- Five years of consistent first-principles AGI research; durable seedbed for later focused projects.
- Range of substrates explored: histogram-precision, attractor dynamics, sparse spiking, VAE convolution kernels, active dendrites.
- Public artifact; specific notebooks (QDL, attractor learning, domain quantization) are cite-worthy standalone investigations.

## Architecture

- Each subdirectory is a self-contained experiment line with its own notebooks (e.g. `Free Energy Minimization Framework/`, `QDL - Quantized Distribution Learning/`, `Attractor Learning/`, `Active Synapse/`, `Predictive Coding/`, `SRU (Spiking Recurrent Unit)/`, `EngramSOM`).
- Numbered notebooks within each line track iterations (`01.ipynb`, `02.ipynb`, …) — seed-and-fork experimentation pattern.
- Year-stamped folders (`2020-1`, `2024`, `2025`, `2026`) capture later, organized sprints.
- No package layout; each notebook runs standalone. Pattern Machine has a 2021 standalone implementation under `Pattern Machine`/`Pattern Machine 2`.

## Chronology

- **2019-07** — Repo seeded with imported notebooks; first iterations on Free Energy Minimization.
