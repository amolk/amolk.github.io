## About

Early exploration to turn the existing `deep-rewrite` Claude skill — which dispatches specialist and meta-assessment passes over a document and then rewrites it — into a standalone paid service. The reference point is reviewer3.com, which monetizes adversarial peer-review of research papers; the same multi-perspective critique loop generalizes to PRDs, design docs, RFCs, and articles. Status: idea / pre-build. No repo yet.

## Why notable

- Direct line from a working personal skill to a potential commercial product — the engine already exists.
- Tests the broader thesis that high-value writing/review workflows can be sold as thin LLM-wrapped services.

## Architecture

- `IDEA.md` — single seed note pointing at reviewer3.com as the inspiration and pricing model.
- Reuses the existing `deep-rewrite` skill (multi-iteration specialist + meta-assessment passes) as the core engine.
- Productization questions: target market (researchers vs. PMs vs. writers), self-serve vs. API, model cost economics.
