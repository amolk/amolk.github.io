A Rails 4.0.0.rc2 scaffold generated October 2013 via `rails_apps_composer` (rails-bootstrap recipe) on Ruby 1.9.3. No domain logic was ever written - the repo is the generator output essentially as produced, with a trivial `visitors_controller`, no models, and a single commit in history.

Included in the portfolio purely for chronological completeness as a marker that an early-Rails-4 scaffold was set up. The generator stack (Bootstrap/Sass + Haml, SQLite, Simple Form, Thin/Unicorn, Guard) is representative of how new Rails apps were spun up in that window, but there's no product behind it.
