CrazyDisk is a native macOS app that scans volumes and folders, visualizes usage as an interactive sunburst, and suggests what to clean up. It is built in Swift and SwiftUI for macOS 14+, ships under the CrazyAIApps label as both a GUI app and a `crazydisk` CLI sharing the same scan core, and is productized for App Store sale at $4.99/year with a 30-day trial and a landing page.

The interesting parts are at the macOS edges - APFS snapshot deletion identity, hidden-space accounting, security-scoped bookmarks for sandboxed access, transactional deletes - plus a clean enough split between scan core, suggestion engine, CLI, and GUI that the same Swift packages drive both surfaces. Active polish through Mar–Apr 2026.
