## About

The v1 Rails application behind the Aflatune music startup — a fully wired music-discovery product covering the track/album/artist domain, playlists, social signals (comments, tips, similarity), and a coming-soon launch flow. Stack is Rails 2.2 with `vendor/rails` vendored in, Sphinx full-text search via Thinking Sphinx, S3 for audio assets, and a role-based authorization plugin. It is not a tutorial app: 27 ActiveRecord models including similarity tables (`track_similarity`, `user_similarity`) and analytics (`track_top_users`, `user_top_tracks`, `track_usage`, `activity_log`) imply a working recommendation/usage pipeline. Archived in mid-2010, superseded by aflatune2.

## Why notable

- Real shipped product, not a scaffold — the model graph (similarity, top-N, activity logs) indicates a working recommendation/analytics layer.
- Period-accurate stack from the Rails 2.x era (vendored Rails, Sphinx, S3) — demonstrates ability to assemble and operate the full early-Rails toolchain.
- Browser-level Watir tests alongside the standard Rails test pyramid.

## Architecture

- `app/controllers/` — 22 controllers spanning accounts, playlists, tracks, comments, admin, sitemap, launch-notification flow.
- `app/models/` — 27 models including `Track`, `Album`, `Artist`, `Playlist`, `PlaylistTrack`, `Genre`, `Source`, `Tip`, `Feedbacks`, plus `*Similarity` and `Top*` analytic models, `UserObserver`, `UserMailer`.
- `vendor/plugins/` — `engines`, `acts_as_commentable`, `acts_as_versioned`, `acts_as_authorizable` (role-based access, "object roles" mixin), Thinking Sphinx.
- `config/environment.rb` — pins `RAILS_GEM_VERSION = '2.2.2'`; `script/server` to run.
- `test/` — `unit/`, `functional/`, `integration/`, plus a `watir/` browser-driven suite (uncommon for the era).
