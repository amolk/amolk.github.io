Carapace Intelligence is a startup cofounded with Kalyan, building decision-intelligence infrastructure for organizations operating in adversarial, multi-party environments. The product treats an organization's situation as a finite directed graph of states and actions, uses LLMs as local judgment elicitors (transition probabilities, outcome scores) rather than global reasoners, and then runs explicit Bayesian expectimax search and sensitivity analysis on top. The result is a ranked set of recommended actions with quantified robustness — an advisor that can surface non-obvious moves and explain why they hold up under perturbation.

Live operational work runs over real geopolitical situations as test subjects, each modeled as a project with per-organization world-models, posture-and-portfolio action spaces, and a stable multi-dimensional state space. Two formal papers landed in May 2026 covering the mathematical pipeline and a characterization of why these games sit outside the reach of classical game theory.
