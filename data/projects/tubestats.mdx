A Rails 3.2.8 scaffold generated September 2012 via `rails_apps_composer`. The name hints at a YouTube-stats idea, but no ingestion or analytics logic was ever built - `app/models/` is empty, only a `home_controller` with an index view exists, and history has a single commit.

Listed here for chronological completeness rather than as a product. The generator stack (Bootstrap/Sass + Haml, MySQL, RSpec + Capybara + FactoryGirl, Simple Form) is a fair snapshot of how Rails 3.2 apps were getting bootstrapped at the time.
