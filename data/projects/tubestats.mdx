A Rails 3.2.8 scaffold generated September 2012 via `rails_apps_composer`, around a YouTube-stats idea, with a `home_controller` over the generated base.

The generator stack - Bootstrap/Sass + Haml, MySQL, RSpec + Capybara + FactoryGirl, Simple Form - is a snapshot of how Rails 3.2 apps were getting bootstrapped at the time.
