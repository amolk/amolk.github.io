## About

A Rails 3.2 edtech app built in 2013 under the Edufied.com brand. Core idea: annotate videos with timestamped paragraphs for educational use. Real product surface — not a generator dump — with versioned content, multi-tier privacy, admin UI, and OAuth (Facebook) login.

## Why notable

- One of the most substantive of the Rails-era projects: real domain modeling (privacy-tiered scopes, slug + infusion-id keys), versioned content, OAuth social login.
- Shipped under a real brand (Edufied.com), not just a side experiment.
- Uses Amol's own published gem (`cache-with-yuicompressor`) — small portfolio echo.

## Architecture

- Rails 3.2+, MySQL, deployed with Capistrano.
- 5 models — `Envideo` (annotated video with paragraphs, privacy levels `[:community, :public, :unlisted, :private]`, slug, infusion id), `User`, `Admin`, `Ability`, `ServerConfiguration`.
- 8 controllers including `envideos`, `admins`, `configurations`, `sitemap`, `static`.
- Auth/authz: Devise + Warden + Omniauth (Facebook) + CanCan; vestal_versions for content versioning.
- Frontend: Haml + Formtastic + jQuery; uses sibling gem `cache-with-yuicompressor` for asset compression.
- Tests: RSpec + Cucumber + autotest.
