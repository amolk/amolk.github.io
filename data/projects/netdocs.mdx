NetDocs was Microsoft's early, ambitious effort at a web-delivered, integrated suite of Office productivity applications - documents, mail, and collaboration delivered through the browser, well ahead of the cloud-Office wave that arrived a decade later. My first work at Microsoft was on this platform.
