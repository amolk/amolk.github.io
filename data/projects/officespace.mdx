As CTO of OfficeSpace.com (2013-2017), I led software development, web infrastructure, and product for a B2B platform serving the commercial-real-estate market. The team built a sophisticated ETL infrastructure capable of synthesizing listing data from a wide range of semi-structured sources, behind a web platform used by over 100,000 business users monthly. The company was later acquired.

The two pieces below - the data backend and the web app - were the heart of it.
