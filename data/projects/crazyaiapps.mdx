CrazyAIApps is the umbrella for a line of native macOS apps that put practical, well-integrated AI into everyday desktop utilities - built as polished, sandboxed Swift/SwiftUI products rather than demos. The apps share a common approach: a fast deterministic core for the everyday path, an optional Claude-powered tier for the harder judgment calls, and privacy-first data handling.

Current apps are [CrazyDisk](/projects/crazydisk/), an AI-assisted disk-usage analyzer, and [CrazyWindows](/projects/crazywindows/), an AI-driven window manager.
