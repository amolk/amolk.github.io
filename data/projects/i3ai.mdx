i3AI - the Infinite Improbability Institute for Artificial Intelligence - is the umbrella under which I publish independent AI research, and this is its public site. It hosts a small library of long-form pages: the HTCC manuscript and supporting material (homeostatic main loop notes, the 2018 poster, chat transcripts), plus essays on cognitive homeostatic agents, the pattern machine, and what conscious AI might mean. The site is the citable home for the research thread that connects the nanogpt experiments, pattern-machine work, and the broader Karta/Carapace line.

Two generations exist. The first was a Pelican (Python) static site from 2024; the current rebuild runs on Sitepress (a Rails-based static-site generator) with Tailwind and Haml/Markdown pages, auto-deployed to Render on push to `main`. The migration aligned the stack with my other Rails work and unlocked richer partials than Pelican allowed.
